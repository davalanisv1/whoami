Black Hole Evaporation & Mass Calculator

I wrote this Python tool to calculate the physical properties of a black hole based on user-input mass. Instead of just showing abstract numbers, the program uses Regular Expressions to handle various units and converts energy release into real-world scales like "Tsar Bombas" and "Hiroshima Bombs" to provide perspective on the magnitude of the physics involved.

# Energy comparison logic from blackholecalc.py
def convert_energy(energy):
    # Comparing energy to the Tsar Bomba (50 Megatons of TNT)
    TSAR_BOMBA_YIELD = 50e3 * 4.184e9 
    tsar_bombas = energy / TSAR_BOMBA_YIELD
    
    return tsar_bombas

print(f"Energy released is equivalent to {tsar_bombas:.3f} Tsar Bombas.")
